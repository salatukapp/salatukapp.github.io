Salatuk is a free, open-source Sunni Islamic companion for daily prayers, Qibla direction, and authenticated remembrances.

Features:
• Five daily prayer times calculated locally on your device using astronomical methods recognized by major Sunni authorities (Muslim World League, ISNA, Egyptian, Umm al-Qura, Karachi, Singapore, Türkiye/Diyanet, Moonsighting Committee, and more)
• Asr juristic rule selector (Standard / Hanafi)
• Auto-detects the appropriate method for your region; manual override available
• Qibla compass using GPS + magnetometer with magnetic-declination correction
• Authenticated daily adhkar — 64 entries across morning, evening, after-prayer, and before-sleep, sourced from Hisn al-Muslim (Sa'id ibn Ali ibn Wahf al-Qahtani) with primary hadith citations and authentication grades
• Scheduled prayer notifications, optional pre-prayer reminder
• Hijri calendar
• Light and dark themes

Privacy
• Zero data collection — nothing leaves your device
• No analytics, no ads, no trackers, no third-party SDKs
• Works fully offline after installation
• Open source — code at https://github.com/omarkaaki/salatuk

Religious accuracy
Every prayer-time calculation method has been cross-verified against three independent reference implementations (Batoul Apps Adhan, PrayTimes.org, Aladhan API). Every adhkar entry includes the primary hadith citation and authentication grade. No weakly-authenticated narrations are included. See RESEARCH.md in the repository for full source documentation.

Free forever. No accounts. No subscriptions. No ads.
