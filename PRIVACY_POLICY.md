# Privacy Policy — Salatuk

**Last updated:** 2026-05-26

## Summary

Salatuk is an Islamic prayer companion app. **We do not collect, transmit, or store any of your personal data on any server.** Everything happens on your device.

## What information the app accesses

### Location
The app requests access to your device's location to:
- Calculate accurate daily prayer times for your geographic position.
- Compute the Qibla direction from your current location toward the Kaaba in Makkah.

Your location is read from the device's GPS / network location services and used **only on your device**. It is never sent to us or to any third party. It is not stored in any persistent file beyond what you have explicitly chosen in settings (e.g., "remember last used city").

You may use the app entirely without granting location permission by entering a city manually in settings.

### Device sensors
The app reads the device magnetometer (compass) to display the Qibla direction. This data never leaves your device.

### Notifications
The app schedules local notifications on your device to alert you at each of the five daily prayer times, if you enable that feature. No push notifications are sent from a server; all alarms are scheduled locally by the operating system.

## What we do **not** collect

- No account, email, name, phone number, or other identifying information.
- No analytics, crash reporting, or telemetry.
- No advertising identifiers.
- No third-party SDKs that transmit usage data.
- No cloud sync.

## Data storage

All app data — your chosen calculation method, location preference, and notification settings — is stored locally on your device using the platform's standard local storage (Android `SharedPreferences`, or your browser's `localStorage` on the web), within the app's private storage area. It is not encrypted at rest beyond the operating system's own app-sandbox protections, and it never leaves your device. Uninstalling the app (or clearing site data in the browser) deletes all of it.

## Children

The app is suitable for all ages and does not collect any data, so it complies with COPPA and GDPR-K by design (no data to be collected from anyone, including children).

## Open source data

The adhkar (remembrances) included in the app are sourced from authenticated Sunni Islamic sources:
- Hisn al-Muslim (حصن المسلم) by Sa'id ibn Ali ibn Wahf al-Qahtani
- Al-Adhkar by Imam al-Nawawi
- Primary hadith collections (Sahih al-Bukhari, Sahih Muslim, Sunan al-Tirmidhi, Sunan Abi Dawud, Sunan Ibn Majah)

Each dhikr in the app includes its hadith source citation.

## Prayer time calculation

The app uses astronomical calculation methods (Umm al-Qura, ISNA, Karachi, Singapore, Turkey/Diyanet, Moonsighting Committee, etc.) recognized by major Sunni Islamic authorities. The calculation runs locally on your device — no internet connection is required after installation.
